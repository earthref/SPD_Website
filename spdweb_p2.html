2 Version history

Pre-v1.0
Draft versions during development.

 

v1.0 – February 24, 2014
The first finalized version of SPD, published along side Paterson, G. A., L. Tauxe, A. J. Biggin, A. J., R. Shaar, and L. C. Jonestrask (2014), On improving the selection of Thellier-type paleointensity data, Geochem. Geophys. Geosyst., doi: 10.1002/2013GC005135.

 

v1.1 – May 01, 2014\\
Changes: \begin{enumerate} \item Corrected minor typographical errors and references. \item Added $\left|\vec{k}^{\prime}\right|$, which is the Arai plot curvature of the best-fit line (Section \ref{sec:Arai}). \item Added numerical tip for the calculation of the noncentral $t$ critical value (Section \ref{sec:Averaging}). \item Added a note that some version of PmagPy and ThellierGUI call $MD_{VDS}$ ``$MD(\%)$'', but their calculation is identical. \end{enumerate}
